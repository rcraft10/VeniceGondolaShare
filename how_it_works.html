Want to know exactly how VeniceGondolaShare.com works?

You simply give us the following information:

  • The dates you are going to be in Venice
  • How many people are in your party
  • Your name and e-mail address so we can contact you in the case of a match

We will then attempt to match you up with people visiting Venice the same time as you.

In the future we will likely allow you to create profiles (if you wish) so you can do your own searching for matches. For example, if you only want to share a ride with another couple around your same age, or that speaks your language, then that will be available to you.

If you have thoughts on how this service could be improved, questions, feature requests, or feedback go ahead and click here to e-mail us!
